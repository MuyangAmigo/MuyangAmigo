## ✨ About Me

I build developer products from idea to launch, with a focus on tools for developers and AI builders. My path runs from engineering at Apple, to marketplace and platform product work at Trip.com, to developer platforms at Microsoft. Today in CoreAI, I work on products that make AI development more approachable, more productive, and more deployable inside the workflows developers already use.

## 🧰 What I Build

- 🤖 AI developer tools that unify model discovery, prompting, debugging, evaluation, and deployment.
- 🛠️ Pro-code platforms that reduce setup friction and help developers move from prototype to production faster.
- 📦 Product experiences that connect strategy, UX, and engineering into launches developers actually notice.

## ⚡ What I Care About

- Developer experience as a product advantage, not an afterthought.
- Shipping tools that close the gap between experimentation and production.
- Turning platform complexity into workflows that feel obvious.

## 📈 Selected Launches

- 🚀 **AI Toolkit for VS Code**: Led product strategy for a unified AI development experience in VS Code, growing usage from **15K to 60K MAU in 6 months** and scaling the product to **1M+ installs**. Shipped major launches across agent building, debugging, evaluation, and deployment.
- 🧩 **Microsoft 365 Agents Toolkit**: Helped build and evolve the pro-code toolkit for Teams, Copilot, and Outlook developers, serving **20K+ monthly active developers** and reaching **443K+ installs**.
- ✈️ **Trip.com Flight Platform**: Led product work on flight booking and post-booking systems serving **400M+ annual users**, including launches for refund and exchange automation plus supplier tooling for **200+ airline partners**.
- 🍎 **Apple Online Store Checkout**: Contributed as an engineer to checkout redesign work, including API optimizations that **reduced calls by 40%**.
